Sir Isaac Newton

English Polymath and Physicist (1642-1727)

Date of Birth

25 December 1642

Place of Birth

Woolsthorpe Manor

Education

  • The King's School (Grantham, England): Arithmetic Studies
  • Trinity College (Cambridge, England): Mathematics & Physics Studies

Achivements

  1. 1665: Discoveries in calculus and the nature of light
  2. 1669: Appointed the Lucasian Professor of Mathematics
  3. 1672: Elected a Fellow of the Royal Society
  4. 1675: Invention of the reflecting telescope
  5. 1703: Newton was elected President of the Royal Society
